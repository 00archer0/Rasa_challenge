# How to apply for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana online in 2025?

Currently in the second phase – 2025 to 2029, the PMAY has set a target of providing around 3 crore houses that will include basic amenities such as gas, electricity, water supply, and toilets. Under this target, around 1 crore pucca houses will be built under PMAY-Urban 2.0 and 2 crore houses will be constructed under PMAY-Gramin. Proposals for the construction of over 3.52 lakh houses have been approved at the meeting of the Central Sanctioning and Monitoring Committee (CSMC) under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana – Urban 2.0 (PMAY-U 2.0) on March 20, 2025. Over 3,52,915 lakh houses under Beneficiary Led Construction (BLC) and Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP) components of PMAY-U 2.0 in 10 States/UTs, namely Andhra Pradesh, Arunachal Pradesh, Bihar, Haryana, Jammu & Kashmir, Odisha, Puducherry, Rajasthan, Telangana, Uttar Pradesh have been sanctioned.

The first phase of PMAY was for nine years from 2015 to 2024 where 118.64 lakh houses have been sanctioned and around 92 lakh houses have already been constructed and delivered to beneficiaries.

## **How to apply for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana offline?**

Applicants can also fill the PMAY offline form at state-run Common Service Centres (CSCs) or banks listed under the PMAY. You will have to pay a fee of Rs 25 to fill the PMAY 2025 registration form. Note that the Ministry of Housing and Urban Affairs (MOHUA) has not given authority to any private entity or person to collect a fee from any beneficiary under this scheme.

## **How to apply for PM Awas Yojana 2025 through bank?**

To apply for the PM Awas Yojana through a bank, the beneficiaries should get in touch with the empaneled bank that gives home loans for credit-linked subsidy schemes. Beneficiaries of the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana 2025 should submit the PMAY filled form with a copy of Aadhaar Card and other supporting documents to the bank.

## **Four verticals under PMAY-U 2.0** 

### **1\. Beneficiary Led Construction (BLC)**

-   EWS beneficiary to construct house on own land
-   Provision of land patta/ rights to the landless by States/UTs
-   Geo-tag the construction stages of the house by beneficiaries.
-   Release of instalment is linked to the construction stage
-   Free of cost statutory approvals, if needed
-   Upgradation of Tenable Slums with housing and infrastructure
-   The BLC vertical of the scheme shall provide financial assistance up to Rs 2.5 lakh to individual eligible families belonging to EWS categories having annual income up to Rs 3 lakh, to construct new pucca houses up to 45 sqm (an all-weather dwelling unit) on their own available land.

### **2\. Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP)**

-   EWS beneficiary to purchase/avail allotted houses in Apartment projects by public/ private sector agencies/ parastatal agencies
-   Redeemable Housing Vouchers for the purchase of houses in whitelisted private sector projects
-   Various State Incentives to public/private projects
-   In-Situ Slum Redevelopment of tenable Slums or Slum Resettlement.
-   Under this vertical, affordable houses of 30-45 sqm carpet area will be constructed by public/private agencies and made available for allotment to the eligible beneficiaries of the EWS category.
-   Financial assistance of up to Rs 2.5 lakh per EWS (annual income up to Rs 3 lakh) flat is provided together by central and state agencies on the purchase price of the property to the EWS beneficiary in AHP projects.  
    

### **3.Affordable Rental Housing (ARH)  
**

Model-1: Utilising existing Government-funded vacant houses by converting them into ARH under PPP mode or by public agencies.  
Model-2: Construct, operate and maintain rental housing by private/public entities for urban poor, working women, employees of industries, industrial estates, institutions, and other eligible EWS/LIG families.

-   The state/UT Governments shall ensure that necessary civic/social infrastructure gaps like water, sewer/septage, sanitation, internal road, community centre, health centre, creche etc. along with desired neighbourhood commercial facilities within the campus as per the requirement are addressed to make these houses liveable.
-   EWS and LIG beneficiaries having annual income of Rs 3 lakh and Rs 6 lakh respectively can avail of this facility.

### **4\. Interest Subsidy Scheme (ISS)**

The subsidy will be provided on home loans sanctioned and disbursed on 01.09.2024 or after to eligible beneficiaries of EWS/LIG and MIG for purchase/ repurchase/construction of houses. Maximum loan value of Rs 25 lakh, Maximum house value of Rs 35 lakh

-   5 Yearly instalments of loan subsidy
-   Annual household income: – EWS – up to Rs 3 lakh, LIG – up to Rs 6 lakh, MIG – up to Rs 9 lakh, and EWS/LIG/MIG- loan subsidy up to Rs 1.80 lakh

## **Who is not eligible for Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana 2025?**

-   People with annual income of more than Rs 18 lakh cannot apply for any PMAY related scheme.
-   People who own a pucca house anywhere in the country. However, an exception to this is enhancement of the existing house if the pucca house is less than 21 sqm.
-   People who have previously taken any government grant to buy a home

-   To know this, log on to the PMAY website at https://pmay-urban.gov.in/ and check PMAY-U 2.0 scheme guidelines.
-   Have a valid Aadhaar card, else you cannot apply for PMAY unit.
-   All the details asked should be filled and should be accurate so that the form is not rejected.

## **What are components of PMAY 2024? (Application form submission closed)**

The deadline for submission of PM Awas Yojana 2024 application was December 31, 20204. There are 4 verticals under which PMAY housing units were available. Note that these verticals have been changed in PMAY-U 2.0.

-   **In-situ Slum Redevelopment (ISSR):** This is slum rehabilitation where the government grants Rs 1 lakh to eligible beneficiaries and redevelops the land under the slums.
-   **Affordable Housing in Partnership (AHP):** In this, states will build affordable housing projects with central assistance of Rs 1,50,000, either through central agencies or in partnership with the private sector for the EWS category.
-   **Beneficiary-led individual house construction/enhancements (BLC):** In this, there are provisions that people, especially from the EWS category, can either construct a new house or renovate the existing house with central assistance of Rs 1,50,000.
-   **Credit Linked Subsidy Scheme (CLSS):** In a bid to reduce the interest and total loan amount for first-time borrowers, the government offers central subsidy on home loans of between Rs 6 lakh and Rs 12 lakh at lower rates of interest, for the construction of new homes or renovation of existing homes.

## **What are the two types under which PMAY was applied in 2024?**

The PMAY scheme whose last date for submission was December 31, 2024, could be applied in two broad categories:

-   **Slum dwellers  
    **Slum dwellers are people living in informal settlements within cities in poor living conditions. These have unhygienic and improper infrastructure including water for drinking and sanitation facilities. As these people have kutcha houses, the slum dwellers could apply for Housing for All under the Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana scheme.
-   **Under other three components**

Under this category, PMAY applicants were divided into three subcategories:

-   -   Economically Weaker Section (EWS)
        -   Up to Rs 3 lakh
        -   6.5%
    -   Lower Income Group (LIG)
        -   Rs 3 – 6 lakh
        -   6.5%
    -   Middle Income Group-1 (MIG-1)
        -   Rs 6 – 12 lakh
        -   4%
    -   Middle Income Group-2 (MIG-2)
        -   Rs 12 – 18 lakh
        -   3%

## **How to apply for PM Awas Yojana 2025 online under other three components?**

-   Log on to https://pmaymis.gov.in/
-   Under Citizen Assessment apply online by choosing benefits under the other 3 components.
-   Enter the Aadhaar number and click submit.
-   Enter all the asked details, enter the captcha code, and click submit.
-   You can edit the PMAY online by accessing your application form by entering the Aadhar card number.  
    To apply PMAY online under the slum dwellers category, choose slum dwellers under the Citizen Assessment option and proceed with the steps as mentioned above.

## **How can slum dwellers apply for PM Awas Yojana 2025 online?**

-   Visit the official Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana at pmaymis.gov.in
-   From the Citizen Assessment dropdown box, choose the ‘For Slum Dwellers’ option.
-   Enter Aadhaar number and click submit. Note that if an incorrect Aadhaar number is entered, the form will be rejected.
-   If the information submitted is correct, you will go to the next page where other information including name, income, number of family members, residential address, mobile number, religion, caste, head of the family.
-   Enter captcha and submit.

### Can I apply for PMAY on my own?

Absolutely, eligible beneficiaries have the option to complete their online registration autonomously through the Ministry's official website.

### Who is eligible for PMAY benefits?

PMAY is applicable to individuals who own kutcha houses and do not possess pucca houses anywhere in India. Furthermore, the applicant's annual income should not exceed Rs 18 lakh. Lastly, it's important that family members have not previously availed of any other government housing scheme.

### Which documents are required for PMAY?

The list of essential address proof documents for PMAY includes the Passport, Voter Card, a document from a public servant verifying your identity and residence, a rental agreement on stamp paper, Residence Address Certificate, Aadhar card, Bank Statements reflecting the address and/or a Life Insurance Policy.

### Can I get a home loan for PMAY from any bank?

Certainly, you have the flexibility to obtain a PMAY home loan from any bank.

### Can I apply for PMAY twice?

No, PMAY does not permit multiple housing applications from the same individual.

### How are beneficiaries identified?

The PMAY scheme follows a demand-driven approach under which states identify eligible beneficiaries through a demand survey, based on specific criteria.

### How is PMAY subsidy released to beneficiaries?

The PMAY subsidy is released by the Central Nodal Agencies (CNAs) based on the disbursements made by primary lending institutes (PLIs) to the beneficiaries. Subsidy, so disbursed by the CNA to the PLI is credited by the PLI to the borrower’s account upfront by deducting it from the principal loan amount. As a result, the borrower will pay EMI on the remainder of the principal loan amount.

### Can I edit my PMAY online form after submission?

No, one cannot edit their PMAY application once it is submitted. This is why all details must be checked before form submission to ensure accuracy. If there's a genuine need for correction, contact the authorities for guidance.

### What is the process after submitting the PMAY online form?

After submitting the form, your application will be verified by officials concerned. If you meet the eligibility criterion, you will be selected for PMAY benefit.

### How can I check the status of my PMAY application after filling out the online form?

You can check the status of your PMAY application online through the official PMAY website using your application reference number.
